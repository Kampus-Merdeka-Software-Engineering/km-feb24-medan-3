    Business Problem:

    How to increase our sales to 20% in one year?

    Hypothesis
    • If we increase the number of vending machines in locations with the highest sales, then sales will increase
    • If we accommodate consumer needs and preferences, then sales will increase
    • If we increase the number of products that are in the most popular categories, then sales will increase
    Metrics
    • Sales
    • Number of product transactions
    • Popular categories/products
    Preview

    -- revou-project-sql-416615.Vending_Machine_Cleaned
    -- H1 If we increase the number of vending machines in locations with the highest sales, then sales will increase
    -- Laporan penjualan bulanan
    SELECT
    EXTRACT(YEAR FROM TransDate) AS Year,
    EXTRACT(MONTH FROM TransDate) AS Month,
    SUM(RQty) AS MonthlySales
    FROM
    revou-project-sql-416615.Vending_Machine_Cleaned.Sales
    GROUP BY
    Year, Month
    ORDER BY Year, Month;
